linear equations

An equation that maximum degree is known as Linear Equatuins. The graph of it is a straight line.

for example

  • y = 8x - 2
  • ax1 +bx2 - cx3 = 9

are some examples of a linear in two and three variables

Slope of intersection of two linear equations

y1 = mx1 + a and

y2 = mx2 + b

for the points P(x1 - y1)and Q(2 , y2) is :

m = (y2 - y1)/ (x2 - x1)
